Friends of the Library

A personal note to all Sherwoodians and Friends of the Library who have supported the Friends and the annual book sale for so many years. We are pleased to announce that because of the success of our book sales on our cart and special shelves in the library we are no longer planning to hold our April book sale. In this way new books will be purchasable on a daily basis as space becomes available. We still need and appreciate your book donations to help fulfill our mission of making books accessible to all at low cost. We also encourage your continued membership to support the Sherwood Public Library.

Be sure to check out the Friends Book Shelf located in the library for great deals on books all year. Cart selection updated regularly.

Thank you to all for supporting the Friends of the Library.
Donation of books can be dropped off year round at the Public Library to help support the book cart.
